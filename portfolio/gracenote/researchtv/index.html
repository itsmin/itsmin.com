    ResearchTV is customer facing, report generation tool. The first build was co-produced by Pivotal Labs and Tribune Media Services.

    After launch of ResearchTV, Tribune made the decision to adopt the "Gracenote" brand for the entertainment data group. Several iterations on the UI along with customer communications were planned to manage this transition.

    There is a small internal team of that engineers that continues to iterate on the application.
